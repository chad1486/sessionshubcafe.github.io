About

Established on November 2016 Session Hub Cafe is the newest place in Baguio to have your sessions, chitchats and precious moments with your family, friends and acquaintances. We offer wide selections of hot and cold drinks, breads, pastries and very comfortable dining area to make you enjoy the whole experience of visiting us. You will find us at 2nd Floor Gamaphel Building, Session Road, Baguio City. (Beside Prime Hotel).
